# 🛡️ TrustScan

**AI-Powered Fraud Detection & Phishing Detector**

TrustScan is an open-source security tool designed to help users identify fraudulent websites, phishing attempts, and scam domains instantly. Powered by **Claude AI**, it analyzes URL patterns, SSL certificates, domain reputation, and brand impersonation signals to provide a comprehensive trust verdict.

## ✨ Features

- **🔍 Instant URL Scanning:** Analyze single URLs for fraud signals.
- **📊 Bulk Scanner:** Paste up to 10 URLs to scan them all at once.
- **🤖 AI Verdict:** Powered by Claude AI to interpret complex risk signals.
- **🔒 Security Checks:** Validates SSL, encryption signals, and domain age.
- **🎣 Phishing Detection:** Identifies brand impersonation patterns.
- **💾 Privacy First:** Scan history is stored locally on your device.
- **🌙 Dark/Light Mode:** Adjustable UI themes.
- **📤 Export & Share:** Export results to PDF or share via WhatsApp/Twitter.
- **🛠️ Extra Tools:** Includes WHOIS Lookup and Email Phishing Checker.
